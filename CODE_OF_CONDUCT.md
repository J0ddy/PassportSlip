# Code of conduct

## Our standard

Participants are expected to communicate respectfully and constructively.

Examples of welcome behaviour:

- using inclusive and considerate language
- accepting constructive feedback
- focusing on what is best for the project
- showing patience with different experience levels

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- harassment or intimidation
- personal attacks
- sexualised language or imagery
- publishing private information without permission
- sustained disruption of discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not meet this standard.

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent restriction from project spaces.
